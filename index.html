✈️ Before You Land

Packing · Airport · First moves
▾
📱Download Before You Go▾
DiDi — Uber backup, sometimes cheaper/faster
Ecobici — bike share. Day pass ~$3 USD
Google Maps — download CDMX offline map
Your US phone plan probably works in Mexico (T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon include it). Check before you leave. If not, buy a Telcel SIM at the airport for ~$15 USD.
🧳Packing List▾
Daytime clothes:
Shorts, t-shirts, sneakers — whatever's comfortable
Walking shoes — you're averaging 15k steps/day
Light jacket — CDMX is 7,300 ft elevation, nights drop to ~50°F
Going out clothes:
2 good shirts (button-up or fitted polo)
Dark jeans or trousers
Clean shoes (nice sneakers or loafers)
Essentials:
Sunscreen — altitude = stronger UV, you WILL burn
Sunglasses
Portable charger — phone will die from Maps + Uber
Cash: withdraw 3,000–4,000 MXN at the airport ATM (~$170-230 USD)
Debit card with no foreign transaction fee (Schwab, etc.)
Passport (obviously)
Imodium / Pepto — you're eating at every street cart we pass. Bring it.
Going out here is like going out in NYC — people dress well. Shorts are fine during the day but don't look chopped at night. Clubs will turn you away.
🛬Airport Landing Checklist▾
At MEX airport, in this order:
Immigration: Fill out the customs form on the plane. Have your passport + return flight info ready
ATM: After baggage claim, hit a bank ATM (Santander, HSBC, Banamex). Withdraw 3,000-4,000 MXN. Decline the "conversion" — always choose pesos. Airport ATMs have the best rates.
Skip the taxi desk. Walk outside to the Uber pickup area
Uber pickup: Terminal 1 → Exit 4. Terminal 2 → Ground Floor, Exit 2. Share your pin with the group chat.
Uber to Airbnb is ~25 min, ~200 MXN ($11 USD). If 2+ of you land together, split one UberXL.
UberXL exists in CDMX but vehicles are smaller than US XLs (tight third row). Fits 5-6 max. Wait times can be 10-15 min longer. For 9 guys, default to 2 UberXL or 3 regular Ubers all trip.
Skip the random taxi guys in the terminal. Uber or nothing.
👔Dress Code Cheat Sheet▾
Daytime: Whatever you want. Shorts, sneakers, t-shirts all fine. Bring layers — mornings and evenings are cool.
Dinners (Fonda Fina, Contramar, Páramo): Smart casual. Clean jeans, a collared shirt or nice fitted tee, good sneakers or loafers. Just don't look chopped.
Handshake Speakeasy: Step it up. Button-down or polo, dark jeans, real shoes. This is the #1 bar in the world.
Clubs (Departamento, Cometa, Fünk): Dark jeans or trousers, fitted shirt, clean shoes. Bouncers DO turn people away. Don't look chopped and you're fine.
Patrick Miller: Anything goes. It's a sweaty 80s dance party.
Lucha Libre: Whatever + your lucha mask.
Xochimilco: Sunglasses, sunscreen. You'll be on a boat for 4 hrs.
When in doubt — dark jeans + a good shirt works everywhere.

🇲🇽 Spanish Cheat Sheet

tap below to go back
Basics
👋 ¿Qué onda? — What's up
🚫 No, gracias — No thanks (street vendors)
🤝 Con permiso — Excuse me (getting through a crowd)
🙅 No hablo español — I don't speak Spanish
👍 Está bien — It's fine / all good
⏳ Ahorita — Right now / in a bit (you'll hear this constantly, means both)
😮 Neta? — Really? / For real?
🤙 Güey — Dude (pronounced "way", you'll hear it every sentence)
Bachelor Party
🎉 Es su despedida de soltero — It's his bachelor party
💍 Él se va a casar — He's getting married (point at Matty)
👰 ¡El novio! — The groom! (introducing Matty)
🍻 Una ronda para el novio — A round for the groom
👥 Somos nueve — There's nine of us
Ordering
🍺 Una ronda de shots para todos — Round of shots for everyone
🍺 Nueve cervezas, por favor — Nine beers please
🥵 Sin picante — No spice
🧾 La cuenta, por favor — The check
💳 ¿Aceptan tarjeta? — Do you take card?
💰 ¿Propina incluida? — Is tip included?
🥃 Un mezcal, derecho — A mezcal, neat
Getting Around
🏠 Campeche cuatro-quince, Hipódromo Condesa — Airbnb address for Ubers
📍 Aquí está bien — Stop here (Uber)
🚻 ¿Dónde está el baño? — Where's the bathroom
💵 ¿Cuánto cuesta? — How much?
Going Out
🥂 ¡Salud! — Cheers
🚪 ¿Hay cover? — Is there a cover?
📋 Reservación para siete / nueve — Reservation for 7 / 9
📋 Estamos en la lista — We're on the list
Xochimilco / Lucha
🚣 ¿Dónde está el embarcadero? — Where's the dock?
🎵 Una canción, por favor — One song (mariachis)
🎭 ¿Cuánto por la máscara? — How much for the mask?
Morning After
💀 Estoy crudo — I'm hungover
☕ Un café, por favor — A coffee please
💧 Agua, por favor — Water please
💊 ¿Tiene Pepto? — Do you have Pepto? (OXXO)
Hype
🔥 ¡Está chingón! — This is awesome (slang)
😱 ¡No mames! — No way! (slang, use with friends)
🚀 ¡Vamos! — Let's go!
